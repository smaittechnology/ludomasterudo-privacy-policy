Ludo Game Rules

Ludo Master follows the classic rules of the ludo game with options for customization. Here are the rules:

  • Players: The game can be played by 2, 3, or 4 players. You can also choose to play against the computer.
  • Colors: Players can choose their colors from the available options before the game starts.
  • Objective: The objective is to move all four of your pieces from the starting point to the home area.
  • Movement: Players take turns rolling a single die to determine the movement of their pieces. A piece can only move out of the starting area when a one or six is rolled.
  • Capturing: If a piece lands on a square occupied by an opponent's piece, the opponent's piece is sent back to their starting area.
  • Winning: The first player to move all their pieces to the home area wins the game.
  • Selecting Numbers: Players can select the number (1 or 6) to get their dice out of the home base.
  • Human Play: Play against human opponents either locally or online.
  • Computer Play: Play against AI opponents with adjustable difficulty levels.
